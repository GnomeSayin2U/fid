Battle Stations

We have a great section of the store called "The Battle Stations"

The battle stations are E-sport recommended Pc setup stations where teams/groups can rent to either practice or play a tournament.
We offer days and times when, on the spot, customers may try out a perticular setup and the power of their components.
We offer tournament nights where customers may enter for either a cash prize or for in-store credit.
we also have Esports certified Employees to host matches for local events, tournaments or even practice matches.
